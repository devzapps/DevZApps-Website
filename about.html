What is Zach Z. Apps?

This is a page about Zach Z Apps and what it is, who is behind it, and my goals and passion that moves my projects forward.

Feel free at anytime to reach out if any questions arise. Having trouble with one of my apps? Would you like to give me a review? You can reach out using this site on the contact page, or on Twitter - @ZachZApps.

The Mission

Zach Z Zapps has two main purposes.
The first, to show off my current, upcoming, and past development projects.
The second, to one day grow into the kind of success where my projects and applications help make someone's life, that much easier.

The Developer

I have always had a passion for computer science. I would say I got into the game late. I got a general Information Technology Degree online from American Public University. From here, I studied and practice to get a couple CompTIA certificates. All the while, searching for jobs that would let me get into my desired field.

Fast forward 4 years and 100s of job applications (with barely an interview), I finally have been able to get into the technology field. On top of that, I have started Zach Z. Apps. This is my gateway into development. I love the ability to grab a keyboard and create. I have always been artistic, but unable to express that through the normal means. I feel development has been that outlet.
