Your Pro Flash Cards

Your Pro Flash Cards. The best way to study for any topic.

Easily create as many decks as you could ever need. Adding cards is a cinch as well, and each deck can have an unlimited amount of cards.

Quickly test your knowledge of a topic in a fast and efficient manner that you can take with you anywhere. No more packs notecards and rubber bands to drop and lose. No more need to sort and shuffle your decks before you study. Your Pro Flash Cards does it all for you!

Here comes another app!

Today I am releasing my second app called "Your Pro Flash Cards"

The idea for a flash cards app came from watching my wife struggle with flash cards while in college. Certain classes, notecards were her best friend when it came to learning certain terms or subjects, as many can attest too. That didn't mean they were convenient or easy to use.

Your Pro Flash Cards was derived from watching the little struggles she had to put up with using notecards. She would constantly have piles of cards from different decks, she would have to manually shuffle and mix them up (which costs precious time), and when the time came she would have to find them. I have yet to mention the kids running around that would constantly get into them, mixing decks up, ripping up cards, coloring over all her hard work, or just plain losing them altogether.

With my app, everything can be kept together in one place, easily manageable and organized. What would normally be hand written on hundreds of cards, can now be in one spot, on your phone, that you can take anywhere to add, edit, or review whenever free time allows.

Your Pro Flash Cards is easy, simple to use, and has many more features on the way.

Currently, features include:

  • Super easy deck management
    • Easy to add decks and cards
    • Easily edit existing cards and deck names
    • Swipe to delete entire decks or individual cards
  • Super fast, Super simple
  • Optimized for any iPhone updated to 13.2 or later
  • Supports iOS Dark Appearance
  • Tutorial deck to help walk through app features
