Letter Practice

My first release!

Letter Practice is an app developed for parents to work with their children that are learning their letters and numbers. Letter Practice lets you choose between capital letters, lower-case letters, and numbers currently up to 20. Letter Practice will shuffle through a category with the press of a button while randomly presenting in a new color and playing a different sound with each press.

App number 1 is here!

As I have stated before, most of my app ideas come from problems that I need solutions to and this is no exception. Even before my daughter entered school, she had to get involved in the tough world of learning not only her letters, but her numbers as well. While in Preschool, she learned her capital letters with ease. She caught on pretty early and had no real issues. Then came the hard partâ€¦lowercase letters. Eventually, she had to dabble in numbers. One to ten, easy. Eleven and on, not so easy.

I developed this app to make it easy to play with her and help her see and recognize her various letters and numbers. Letter Practice has 3 main categories. Capital letters, lowercase letters, and numbers ranging from one to 20. With the tap of a single button, Letter Practice will shuffle within the category selected and present a letter or number from that category while playing a fun sound and changing to a random color of the rainbow to help keep the kiddos engaged. Presenting a greater challenge, you can also shuffle between all categories at once.

This has been a huge success in my house and now I extend it to you, for free. Have fun with this app and enjoy playing with, and watching your child grow and learn in an easy, and fun environment.
